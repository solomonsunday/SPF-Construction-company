WHAT YOU SHOULD KNOW

Who We Are

SFP has operated in the informal sector for 4 years focusing on small housing development and mid- range public buildings. In 2018 SFP became a Limited Liability Company focusing not only on small scale residential building but with move into medium scale to luxury residential and commercial developments and civil engineering solutions.

What We Offer

The partners of SFP have over 40 years of construction experience in Nigeria and overseas. We pride ourselves in our integrity and commitment to quality, safety and our ability to deliver projects economically for our clients. Our experience in Nigeria has included the delivery of some of the recent visually outstanding projects in Victoria Island and Ikoyi areas of Lagos State. Overseas we have also been involved in the design, project management and construction of several high-profile civil engineering projects.
